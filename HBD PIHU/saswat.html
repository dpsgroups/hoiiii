SASWAT

Priyanka biswayare kana au kahibi. Gote heera te mo jhia, ta chali chalana, bebhara au Guna bhari uncha starara.

Se ekk uchakoti ra narii. Mu ta sanga hai thiba ru nijaku garbita anubhaba kare.

Au besi kahile, mora micha handi phati jiba. Bhagawan tara mangala karantu.

।। ॐ ऐं ह्रीं क्लीं चामुण्डायै विच्चे ।।
